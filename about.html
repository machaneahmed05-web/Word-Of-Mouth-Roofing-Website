Our Story

Two Veterans. One Vision. A Roofing Company Built Differently.

Word of Mouth Roofing was co-founded by Denver Blanscett and Johnny Garza — two roofing professionals who each spent over two decades building their careers and reputations from the ground up.

Denver Blanscett brings more than 20 years of executive-level experience to the table. As a former Vice President at a national roofing design firm, Denver oversaw large-scale residential and commercial projects across the country, developed industry-leading installation standards, and built relationships with the top roofing manufacturers in the business. He knows what quality looks like from every angle — and he won't accept anything less.

Johnny Garza is the backbone of every project Word of Mouth Roofing takes on. With 20+ years of hands-on field experience, Johnny has personally installed, repaired, and replaced thousands of roofs across South Texas. His deep technical knowledge, attention to detail, and commitment to clean, professional jobsites sets the tone for every crew that works under the Word of Mouth name.

Together, Denver and Johnny bring over 40 years of combined roofing expertise to every project. In 2023, they made the decision to stop working for other people's companies and start doing things the right way — their way. Word of Mouth Roofing was born from a shared frustration with an industry that too often puts profits over people, and a shared belief that San Antonio homeowners deserve better.

"We started this company because we knew we could do it better," says Denver. "Not just the roofing — but the communication, the respect, the follow-through. When you work with us, you're not a ticket number. You're a neighbor."

Our Services

Complete Roofing & Exterior Solutions for San Antonio Homeowners

Word of Mouth Roofing is a full-service residential roofing and exterior contractor. Whether you need a complete roof replacement after a storm, a routine inspection, a targeted repair, or a fresh coat of paint to boost curb appeal — we have the expertise and the crew to handle it all under one roof (pun intended). Our eight core services cover every aspect of your home's exterior protection:

  • Roof Replacement — Full tear-off and replacement using premium shingle systems from GAF and Owens Corning. We handle everything from permits to final cleanup.
  • Roof Installation — New construction and addition roofing services. We work with builders and homeowners to deliver flawless new installs on schedule.
  • Roof Repair — Leak patching, flashing repair, damaged shingle replacement, storm damage fixes. We diagnose the root cause and fix it right the first time.
  • Roof Maintenance — Preventative maintenance programs to extend the life of your existing roof, catch small problems before they become expensive ones, and keep your warranty valid.
  • Roof Inspection — Detailed roof inspections for homeowners, buyers, sellers, and insurance claims. You'll receive a full written report with photos and our honest recommendation.
  • Siding — Vinyl, fiber cement, and wood siding installation and replacement. A new exterior starts with the right siding material installed correctly.
  • Exterior Painting — Professional exterior painting services to refresh, protect, and transform the look of your home. Available as a standalone service or bundled free with a full roof replacement.
  • Gutters & Downspouts — Seamless gutter installation, gutter repair, gutter guards, and downspout rerouting to protect your foundation and landscaping from water damage.
