Word of Mouth Roofing is a premier, family-owned roofing company proudly serving San Antonio and surrounding communities. Founded by Denver Blanscett and Johnny Garza — two roofing veterans with over 40 years of combined experience — our mission is simple: deliver the quality roof your home deserves, on time and on budget, every single time. We specialize in residential roof replacement, repair, installation, maintenance, roof inspections, siding, gutters, and exterior painting.

Before launching Word of Mouth Roofing, Denver served as Vice President at one of the nation's leading roofing design firms, overseeing thousands of projects across the country. Johnny brings two decades of hands-on field expertise, from complex roof replacements to full-scale exterior renovations. Together, they built this company on a straightforward principle: treat every homeowner the way you'd want your own family treated.

In just two years, Word of Mouth Roofing has earned a reputation as one of San Antonio's most trusted roofing contractors — not through advertising, but through exceptional craftsmanship and genuine care for every client. When your neighbors recommend us, that's the highest honor we can receive.
