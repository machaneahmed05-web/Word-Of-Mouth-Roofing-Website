Serving Greater San Antonio

Your Local Roofing Experts Across South-Central Texas

Word of Mouth Roofing provides premium residential roofing services across greater San Antonio and the surrounding communities of Bexar County and beyond. If you're a homeowner in South-Central Texas, we're your local roofing experts. Our team of certified roofing professionals is ready to serve you — whether you're in a San Antonio suburb, a Hill Country community, or a small Texas city hours from downtown. We bring the same standards, the same materials, and the same commitment to quality to every community we serve.

Founded by Denver Blanscett and Johnny Garza — two roofing veterans with a combined 40+ years of experience — Word of Mouth Roofing was built to deliver San Antonio's best roofing services to every homeowner in our region. We're licensed and insured across the state of Texas, GAF and Owens Corning certified, and A+ accredited with the Better Business Bureau. No matter where you live in our service area, you can count on Word of Mouth Roofing to show up on time, do the job right, and treat your home like it's our own.
