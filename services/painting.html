Exterior & Interior Services

Give Your Home A Fresh Coat Of Paint

A professional paint job does more than improve curb appeal — it protects your home from the elements and dramatically increases its value. Word of Mouth Roofing delivers premium painting services across San Antonio with expert prep and lasting results.

Get A Free Estimate (210) 206-0779

Whether you're refreshing your home's exterior after a roof replacement, preparing to sell, or simply ready for a change, Word of Mouth Roofing's professional painting crew delivers flawless results every time. We use premium, weather-resistant paints specifically formulated for the Texas climate — products that stand up to blazing summer heat, UV radiation, and the occasional winter freeze. Our painting process begins with thorough surface preparation — power washing, priming, and caulking — because a perfect paint job starts before a single brushstroke is applied.

What We Offer

Interior And Exterior Painting Services

Exterior Painting

Complete exterior house painting using premium, UV-resistant paints. We prep every surface — pressure washing, scraping, priming, caulking — before applying a flawless, durable finish that lasts years.

Interior Painting

Fresh interior walls transform how your home feels. Our interior crews work cleanly and efficiently, protecting your floors and furnishings while delivering smooth, even coverage in any color you choose.

Deck & Fence Staining

Restore the beauty of your outdoor wood with professional deck and fence staining. We clean, sand, and apply penetrating stains that protect against moisture, UV damage, and foot traffic.

Trim & Accent Painting

The right trim color elevates your entire exterior. We handle all fascia, soffit, window trim, shutters, and decorative accents with precision and a sharp eye for detail.

Built To Last

Long-Lasting Painting Options

Not all paint is created equal — and in San Antonio's intense climate, the products we choose matter as much as the technique. Word of Mouth Roofing uses only premium-grade exterior coatings from trusted brands, applied by experienced professionals who know how to make them last.

  • Premium exterior paints with 10–15 year fade and mildew resistance
  • Full surface preparation including power washing, sanding, and priming
  • Caulking of all gaps, cracks, and seams before painting
  • UV-resistant formulas designed for Texas heat and sun exposure
  • Free color consultation — we'll help you pick the perfect palette
  • Clean, respectful crews who protect your landscaping and property
