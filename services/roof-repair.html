What We Fix

Roofing Repair Services

Word of Mouth Roofing handles every type of roofing repair — from minor fixes to major storm damage restoration across San Antonio and surrounding communities.

Shingle Replacement

We replace missing, cracked, curling, and deteriorated shingles with perfectly matched materials — restoring both function and appearance to your roof without a full replacement.

Leak Detection & Repair

Our technicians use advanced inspection techniques to trace water intrusion to its true source — even when the entry point is far from the visible damage — and seal it permanently.

Flashing Repair

Damaged or improperly installed flashing around chimneys, skylights, vents, and roof penetrations is a leading cause of leaks. We re-seal, re-bed, or fully replace flashing to stop water entry.

Ventilation Repair

Poor roof ventilation causes heat and moisture buildup that shortens shingle life and drives up energy bills. We repair and optimize ridge vents, soffit vents, and attic airflow systems.

Storm Damage Repair

Hail, high winds, and falling debris can cause extensive roof damage in minutes. Our storm damage specialists respond quickly to assess, document, and repair all storm-related roofing issues and assist with insurance claims.

Emergency Roof Repair

When a severe storm or sudden event creates an urgent roof failure, we offer priority emergency response to protect your home immediately with temporary protection and rapid permanent repairs.

Don't Ignore the Signs

Are Roofing Problems Impacting Your Home?

Many of the most costly roof repairs could have been minor fixes if caught early. San Antonio homeowners should be on alert for these warning signs that indicate your roof needs immediate professional attention:

  • Water stains or brown spots appearing on ceilings and interior walls
  • Missing, cracked, or displaced shingles following a storm
  • Granules accumulating in your gutters or downspouts
  • Sagging or soft spots in the roof deck when walked on
  • Daylight visible through the attic boards or rafters
  • Moss, algae, or dark streaking across shingle surfaces
  • Flashing that is bent, separated, or corroded around chimneys and vents
