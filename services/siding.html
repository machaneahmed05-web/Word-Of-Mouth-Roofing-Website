Your home's siding is its first line of defense against rain, wind, heat, and pests. When siding becomes damaged, warped, or outdated, it doesn't just hurt curb appeal — it can lead to moisture intrusion, energy loss, and structural damage. Word of Mouth Roofing provides professional siding installation, replacement, and repair services for homeowners across San Antonio and greater Bexar County. Our expert team works with all major siding materials to deliver a beautiful, durable exterior that protects your home for decades to come.

What We Offer

Siding Services

Vinyl Siding Installation

Durable, low-maintenance vinyl siding in a wide range of colors and profiles. Vinyl resists moisture, fading, and insects — an excellent choice for San Antonio's climate.

Fiber Cement Siding

Premium fiber cement siding offers the look of wood with superior durability. Resistant to fire, moisture, and pests — ideal for homeowners who want lasting beauty without the upkeep.

Wood Siding

Classic wood siding brings timeless character to any home. We install and finish wood siding with precision and use high-quality sealers to maximize longevity in the Texas heat.

Siding Repair & Replacement

Cracked, warped, or storm-damaged siding? We match your existing material and complete seamless repairs that restore your home's appearance and protection.

Storm Damage Siding

Hail and high winds cause significant siding damage across San Antonio. We work directly with your insurance carrier to document damage and complete the repair efficiently.

Trim & Fascia

Proper trim and fascia installation frames your siding beautifully and seals the edges of your exterior. We install, repair, and replace trim for a polished, finished look.

More Than Curb Appeal

Your Home's Siding Impacts More Than Just Appearance

Most homeowners think of siding as a cosmetic feature — but it plays a critical structural and functional role in your home's performance. Worn, damaged, or improperly installed siding costs you money every day in ways that aren't always obvious.

  • Energy efficiency — Damaged siding allows conditioned air to escape, driving up your utility bills
  • Moisture protection — Properly sealed siding keeps water out of your walls and foundation
  • Curb appeal & resale value — New siding consistently delivers one of the highest ROIs of any home improvement
  • Pest prevention — Gaps and cracks in siding provide entry points for insects and rodents
  • Structural integrity — Moisture intrusion behind damaged siding causes rot and mold in wall framing

When you invest in new siding from Word of Mouth Roofing, you're not just improving how your home looks — you're protecting what's inside it.
